Pro Tips & Shortcuts

Keyboard Shortcuts
Clean & Save: Ctrl + Shift + S
Standard clean. Saves tabs and frees memory.
Dashboard (Links): Ctrl + Shift + L
Opens your saved sessions library.
Reset (Kill All): Ctrl + Shift + K
Kills tabs in the Active Window only (ignores Scope settings). No saving.

Shortcuts can be customized in your browser's extension settings.
